About Us

Genesis of Robotics is a primarily homeschooled team based in Lewisville, which competes through BEST Robotics. They are a team of engineers, artists, and designers.
Most importantly, they're a team.

The design engineers plan a robot to complete the competition's challenge, and the build engineers manufacture that design.
